About KidsAI

Stephen Parkin, founder of KidsAI Explained, helps families make sense of AI in everyday life. A former teacher and children’s mental health worker, he shows parents how to explain AI simply, set safe habits, and spark creativity and critical thinking so kids grow up confident and future-ready.
